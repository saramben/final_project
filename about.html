Hi everyone! My name is Sara Bennett and I am a lifelong lemonade enthusiast! There is nothing more exciting to me than the opportunity to try a refreshing new lemonade. My friends know me as predictable: any restaurant we go to I will be ordering a lemonade! I hope you all learn to love lemonade just as much as I do!

Fun Facts!

  • Favorite Lemonade Brand: Simply Lemonade
  • Favorite Lemonade Related Song: "She's So Gone" from Lemonade Mouth
  • As an RA in my dorm, I hosted an event where we watched Lemonade Mouth and had 5 types of lemonade
  • I have a private Snapchat story where I review lemonades for my friends
